Executive Summary

Dear Mr. Grant,

Thank you for the opportunity to present this proposal. Battery-powered motorized blinds deliver a premium guest experience, but their ongoing maintenance — manually swapping and charging batteries room by room — places a quiet but persistent burden on your housekeeping and facilities teams.

This proposal outlines a Solar-Powered Blind Charging System designed to eliminate that burden entirely. Each unit harvests ambient and direct light to keep blind batteries continuously topped off, removing the need for manual intervention and reducing the risk of batteries running flat mid-stay.

We propose a 5-unit pilot program to validate performance across a sample of your rooms before any larger commitment — with a clear path to full-property rollout.

02
Product Overview

How It Works

Each unit is a compact, self-contained charging module that installs discreetly alongside your existing motorized blind system. A solar panel — sized for typical window exposure — feeds an onboard charge controller that maintains optimal battery voltage at all times.

☀️

Continuous Solar Harvest

Captures both direct sunlight and diffuse indoor ambient light through an efficient MPPT charge controller, maintaining battery health year-round.

🔋

Intelligent Charge Management

Onboard current sensing prevents overcharge and deep discharge, extending the service life of your blinds' batteries significantly.

🔧

Low-Profile Installation

Designed to integrate cleanly with existing window treatments. No major modifications to walls, frames, or existing blind hardware required.

📈

Scalable Architecture

Each unit operates independently. A pilot of 5 rooms can expand to every room in the property with no systemic changes.
